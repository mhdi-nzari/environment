The Gilman Fund

Together, we can.

Your gift to The Gilman Fund is an investment in the transformative experience of a Gilman education.

Each year, Gilman relies on the collaborative giving of its parents, alumni, and friends to support The Gilman Fund, a critical source of revenue for the School that has an immediate impact on the Gilman experience for every boy, every day. Gifts to The Gilman Fund fuel the exceptional learning opportunities across academics, athletics, and the arts that provide our students with the skills and ability they will need to make a difference in the world.

Together, we can unlock extraordinary possibilities and develop the next generation of curious, confident, and compassionate Greyhounds. Please consider a gift today.

Your Support Makes a Difference!

WHERE YOUR SUPPORT GOES

The Gilman Fund supports the School's mission-critical teacher-coach-mentor model, new and innovative programming, faculty professional development, and more. Unrestricted gifts are the highest priority, allowing the School the flexibility to distribute funds where they're needed most.

Founded in 1897, Gilman School is a pre-kindergarten through twelfth grade independent school located in Baltimore, Maryland. A diverse community dedicated to educating boys in mind, body, and spirit, Gilman seeks to produce men of character and integrity who have the skills and ability to make a positive contribution to the communities in which they live and work.
