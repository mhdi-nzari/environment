Developing a Lifelong Love of Learning

Developing a Lifelong Love of Learning

The talented and dedicated faculty at Gilman support each boy in developing a strong character, a lifelong love of learning, and the drive to be his best self. By graduation, Gilman men have become critical thinkers, thoughtful creators, and innovative problem solvers who attend top-tier colleges and go on to make positive contributions in their careers and communities.

Gilman's Lower School helps boys develop foundational knowledge, learning skills, and personality traits necessary to prepare them for success in school and beyond. We nurture the innate curiosity, wonder, and desire to learn in each boy.

As boys begin to spread their wings in the Middle School, they discover their interests in an encouraging, cooperative, and caring environment. They learn to take risks as well as responsibility while building study, communication, and leadership skills through community involvement, teamwork, and physical fitness.

In Upper School, we prepare young men to make a positive difference in our world. Students engage in a college-preparatory program that emphasizes a strong work ethic, critical thinking skills, effective oral and written communication, and lifelong enjoyment of learning. Gilman offers the best of both worlds: single-sex education combined with coeducation in the junior and senior years.

Preparing Boys for Their Future

Preparing Boys for Their Future

Applying to college is an extension of the Gilman educational experience: a journey of reflection, growth, self-empowerment, and discovery. Gilman graduates are well-prepared to become successful undergraduates at some of our country's most prestigious colleges and universities. Eighty-three percent of the class of 2022 attend a Top 200 School ranked by The Wall Street Journal.

Gilman boys and their families have the advantage of a strong College Counseling Office staffed with four full-time counselors to guide them through the college application process. The College Counseling Office provides students with the tools, information, and support necessary to identify their goals and pursue them with confidence. They help each young man find his best fit by identifying colleges where he will flourish intellectually, socially, and emotionally, both in and out of the classroom.

Gilman boasts a strong global network of more than 8,500 alumni. From innovators and entrepreneurs to doctors and educators, Gilman graduates have the skills and abilities to make a positive difference in our world. Many recall the strong, lifelong relationships with teachers, mentors, coaches, and classmates and building excellent character as foundations for their success. Our talented alumni stay actively connected with the School — and with each other — through networking opportunities, speaking engagements, social gatherings, and more.
