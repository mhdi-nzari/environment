Welcome Back, Alumni!

Welcome Back!

Gilman School has launched thousands of young men into the world since 1897. No matter the path our graduates choose, they are united by an exceptional education of mind, body, and spirit. Their devotion to and appreciation for their alma mater is without equal.

Here we celebrate the School's outcomes and cherish the common ground that serves as a throughline for generations of alumni. Use this site to find out about activities and gatherings where alumni can connect and network, recent Gilman news, opportunities to give back, and so much more. Contact us at alumni@gilman.edu with updates, suggestions, and feedback.

Go Greyhounds!

Andrew Robinson
Director of Alumni Relations

Founded in 1897, Gilman School is a pre-kindergarten through twelfth grade independent school located in Baltimore, Maryland. A diverse community dedicated to educating boys in mind, body, and spirit, Gilman seeks to produce men of character and integrity who have the skills and ability to make a positive contribution to the communities in which they live and work.
